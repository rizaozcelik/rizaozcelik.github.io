Employment 👨‍🔬

 
 
 
 
 

Great Minds Research Intern

IBM Research

June 2021 – November 2021 Zurich, Switzerland
Discovering new research fields and environments is a joy for me. For 6 months, I tasted processing the language of IBM’s AppConnect customers and created a pipeline which allows users to create application flows via natural text. I also created a type-ahead component to hint users while typing and backed the pipeline with a knowledge graph 🧠. The knowledge graph contained the applications in AppConnect and I also implemented a link prediction model to represent relations between applications and their components.

Research Assistant

The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey, TÜBİTAK

December 2018 – June 2019 İstanbul, Turkey
